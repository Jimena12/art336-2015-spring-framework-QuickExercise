Jimena Del Castillo

Graphic Designer

Here is a brief story of who I am I grew up in a city in Peru called Arequipa, came with my family to live in the US and to persue my education. After considering different options, I decided that designing was my vocational path and I should persue a career where I could develop my creativity and design skills. I love to explore different cultures, travel and interact with different people. The source of my inspiration comes from sightseeing around the world and always trying new things, even the ones that I am not used to. Overall, design not only opens the door of creativity, but also helps me to explore social communication ways and makes me create awareness to the public of many of today's issues.
