Jimena Del Castillo

Graphic Designer

I am Jimena Del Castillo, a Peruvian-born graphic designer and artist based in Maryland. I love exploring vibrant and colorful concepts, and have a deep passion for urban art and culture. I currently study Graphic Design at UMBC.

I strive to find the right balance between aesthetics, graphic and web design. Furthermore, I love to explore new ideas were design and clear visuals interact to reach any audience.
